Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Achieve is built with your privacy in mind. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Data we collect

Achieve stores your habits, check-ins, notes, affirmations, and coach conversations locally on your device. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

If you use the AI Coach feature, messages may be sent to a third-party AI provider to generate responses. These messages are used only to provide coaching and are not used to train models or build advertising profiles.

How we use your data

  • To display your habits, streaks, and weekly progress
  • To send optional reminders you configure in the app
  • To power AI Coach conversations when you choose to use that feature

Data storage

Your habit data stays on your device unless you explicitly enable a sync or backup feature. We do not share your personal habit data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your choices

You can delete habits, notes, and conversation history within the app at any time. Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data from your device.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at contact@achievehabit.com.
